Embark on a photography adventure with our SAAS web application. Seamlessly blending React, Node.js, MongoDB, and social media APIs, we provide photographers with a platform to showcase their work, engage in contests, and connect with their audience. From client dashboards to admin control, our comprehensive solution fosters creativity and interaction.

As a dedicated software engineer, I undertook the exhilarating challenge of developing a SAAS web application that fosters a vibrant community of photographers. This innovative platform not only facilitates seamless photo uploads but also empowers photographers to engage in contests, receive user feedback, and share their work on social media. My responsibilities encompassed the creation of both client and admin dashboards, demonstrating my versatile skill set. Leveraging an extensive array of technologies, including React, Node.js, Bootstrap, jQuery, HTML, CSS, MongoDB, Docker, CI/CD, AWS, and various social media APIs, I contributed to building a thriving and interactive photography ecosystem.

Key Modules and Contributions:

1. Photographer Community Platform:

  • At the heart of this SAAS application lies a robust platform that brings photographers together. I architected modules that enable seamless photo uploads, categorization, and interaction with user-generated content.

2. Contests and Engagement Features:

  • I designed and implemented the contest module, allowing photographers to participate, while users could upvote, comment, and share the submitted photos on social media. This module emphasizes my expertise in fostering user engagement.

3. Client and Admin Dashboards (React, Bootstrap):

  • I meticulously developed intuitive client and admin dashboards. The client dashboard allows photographers to manage their portfolios, participate in contests, and engage with their audience. The admin dashboard empowers administrators to curate contests, moderate content, and manage user interactions.

My Responsibilities:

Full-Stack Development:

I undertook a comprehensive role, creating both the frontend and backend components of the SAAS application.

Dashboard Design:

I crafted intuitive client and admin dashboards, using React and Bootstrap to ensure a user-friendly experience.

Engagement and Contests:

I designed the contest module, fostering user engagement through voting, commenting, and sharing.
